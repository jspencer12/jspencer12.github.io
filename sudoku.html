The Jonathan and Friends New York Times Sudoku Log

I really enjoy sudoku! I just got into it early 2023 and have dedicated an unhealthy amount of time to it since then. Since I play it every day and track my own scores anyway, I thought it would be fun to make a way for my friends to track their scores, since the NYTimes app doesn't have that function. This is a work in progress, but if you want to participate, just log your scores in the form below and watch them pop up in the table. (The table should republish every few minutes, but if you get impatient you can go directly to the sheet.) I'll add more tables/analytics in the future!
